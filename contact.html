Content Creator • Developer • Writer

Diego (Overgrown) is a Puerto Rican developer and content creator, currently residing in Newark, Delaware. While studying Computer Science at Harvard University through the CS50 program, he began publishing game modifications for the popular game Minecraft. He created his very own speculative fiction story at the age of 16, and has been developing now as an expirienced writer. An avid learner, he's been developing his skills as a coder, picking up C, Java, and SQL at a fast rate and creating 55 diffirent game modifications for Minecraft in just 3 months. He has slowly garnered a very small following through his creative projects and unorthodox humor.

He has an avid interest in video games and RPG lore, constantly diving into Destiny, Valorant, Wynncraft, and many other RPGs to learn more about their story and world. He is also a photographer and athlete.
